Academic Scholarship

Academic scholarship is financial support for students who achieve excellent academic results.

Requirements:
  • Active full-time student status
  • Minimum of 23 credits completed in courses included in the scholarship average
  • Minimum GPA of 3.80
  • Not having exceeded the eligibility period assigned to the program duration
How it is calculated:

The scholarship amount is determined based on ranking, which is calculated from the academic average and completed credits. Average calculation: [(1.2 × required subject credit × grade) + (elective subject credit × grade)] / total credits enrolled

Corvinus Scholarship

The Corvinus scholarship recognizes students who achieve the most outstanding academic results.

Requirements:
  • Participation in full-time studies
  • Exceptional academic results (typically the best students of the program)
  • Automatic award based on program ranking
  • Additional specific requirements may vary by program
Important information:

The number and proportion of Corvinus scholarship places vary by program. Scholarship recipients are automatically placed at the top of the ranking based on their academic performance.

Regular Social Scholarship

Regular social scholarship supports students in difficult financial situations.

Requirements:
  • Active student status
  • Proof of social need
  • Submission of income and property statement
  • Not having exceeded the eligibility period assigned to the program duration
Application:

Applications for regular social scholarship can be submitted each semester by the specified deadlines. Required documents must be submitted through the student portal.

Details in regulations Social Guide

Extraordinary Social Scholarship

Extraordinary social scholarship provides assistance in unexpected, exceptional life situations.

Requirements:
  • Unexpected, exceptional life situation (e.g., death of family member, serious illness, natural disaster)
  • Active student status
  • Documentation of the extraordinary event
  • Justification in the application
Application:

Applications for extraordinary social scholarship can be submitted at any time after the event occurs. Applications are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Details in regulations

Dormitory Social Scholarship

Dormitory social scholarship supports students living in dormitories who are in financial need.

Requirements:
  • Dormitory accommodation
  • Proof of social need
  • Submission of income and property statement
  • Active student status
Application:

Applications for dormitory social scholarship can be submitted during the dormitory admission period or each semester. The scholarship amount is reflected as a reduction in dormitory fees.

Details in regulations

Public Affairs Scholarship

Public affairs scholarship recognizes and supports students who actively participate in university public life.

Requirements:
  • Active student status
  • Regular, active participation in university public life (Student Union, residential college work, university organizations, etc.)
  • Proof of minimum monthly time commitment
  • Documentation of public affairs activities
Application process:

Applications for public affairs scholarship can be submitted each semester. The application must detail the public affairs activities, work performed, and time commitment. Applications are evaluated by a committee.

Important information:

The purpose of the public affairs scholarship is to recognize active participation in building the university community. The scholarship amount may vary depending on the quantity and quality of work performed.
